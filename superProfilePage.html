Order Reviews

Review some orders to see how well your staff are counting. Check their accuracy on the counts as well as their efficiency, such as time to complete orders, time away from the chute during orders, etc.

Performance Reviews

Much like with the accuracy and efficiency for counting, check up on your staff to see how they are doing overall. Identify where they are lacking and where they are excelling with the Guided Performace Review.
